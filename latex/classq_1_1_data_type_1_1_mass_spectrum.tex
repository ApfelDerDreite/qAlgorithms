\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
A class to store mass spectrum data. 

The \doxylink{classq_1_1_data_type_1_1_mass_spectrum}{Mass\+Spectrum} class is used to store mass spectrum data. It contains the intensity, mz, ionization mode, measurement mode, and MS level of the mass spectrum. ~\newline
 
\begin{DoxyParams}{Parameters}
{\em intensity} & The intensity of the MS signal \\
\hline
{\em mz} & The mass-\/to-\/charge ratio of the MS signal \\
\hline
{\em ionization\+Mode} & The ionization mode, i.\+e. whether the signal is from positive or negative mode \\
\hline
{\em measurement\+Mode} & The measurement mode, i.\+e. whether the signal is profile or centroid \\
\hline
{\em ms\+Level} & The MS level, i.\+e. whether the signal is from MS1 or MS2 \\
\hline
\end{DoxyParams}
